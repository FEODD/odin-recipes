ODIN RECIPES

  • Total time:45 mins

  • Ingredients:

    1. 3/4 lb. ground chicken
    2. 1/2 lb. chicken sausage, casings removed
    3. 1/3 c. freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
    4. 2 tsp. dried oregano
    5. 3 cloves garlic, minced
    6. 1 large egg
    7. Kosher salt
    8. Freshly ground black pepper
    9. Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
