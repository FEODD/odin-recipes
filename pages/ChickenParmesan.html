ODIN RECIPES

  • Total time:45 mins

  • Ingredients:

    1. 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
    2. salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
    3. 2 large eggs
    4. 1 cup panko bread crumbs, or more as needed
    5. ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
    6. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, or more if needed
    7. ½ cup olive oil for frying, or as needed
    8. ½ cup prepared tomato sauce
    9. ¼ cup fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
    10. ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
    11. ½ cup grated provolone cheese
    12. 2 teaspoons olive oil
